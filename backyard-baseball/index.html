Backyard Baseball — Free Home Run Derby Game

A free browser sports game at Dad Arcade. Press Space to swing. Time it perfectly to crush home runs with bobblehead players.

About This Game ▾

Home Run Derby with three difficulty levels, instant feedback on every swing, slow-motion replay on home runs, a distance progress meter with milestone goals, bobblehead characters with spring-physics heads, and fireworks celebrations. Track your best distance on the per-difficulty leaderboard.
