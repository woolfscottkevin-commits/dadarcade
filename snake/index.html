Snake — Free Online Arcade Game

A free browser arcade game at Dad Arcade. Arrow keys or WASD to steer. Eat food, grow longer, collect power-ups, chase the high score.

About This Game ▾

Features three difficulty levels (Easy with wall wrap and a continue, Normal, and Hard with obstacles), four power-up types, rainbow mode, milestone celebrations, and smooth interpolated movement. Compete against your own high score on each difficulty.
