Paddle Duel — Retro Paddle-and-Ball Arcade

Move your paddle to hit the ball past the computer. First to 11 points wins the match.

About This Game

How to Play
Use the arrow keys (or W/S) to move your paddle up and down. Hit the ball past the computer's paddle to score. The ball speeds up with each volley and angles differently depending on where it hits your paddle. First player to 11 wins!

Features

  • Retro-style visuals — dotted center line, blocky paddles, chunky score digits
  • 3 difficulty levels: Easy, Normal, Hard
  • Daily and all-time leaderboards
  • Ball speed increases with each rally
  • Paddle hit position affects ball angle
  • Retro sound effects
  • Touch controls for mobile

Controls

  • Up/Down Arrow or W/S — Move paddle
  • P — Pause
  • M — Mute
  • Touch — Drag on left half of screen to move paddle
