Arcade Shooter Ages 6+

Defend Earth from alien invaders in this classic arcade shooter, rebuilt for the modern browser. Math Quest RPG

RPG Educational Ages 6-14 Addition Subtraction Multiplication Division

Math Quest RPG turns math practice into an adventure. Choose from three character classes — the tanky Warrior, the powerful Mage, or the versatile Ranger — and dive into procedurally generated dungeons. Every battle is a math problem: answer correctly to attack, answer fast for bonus damage. Problems scale from simple addition on Floor 1 to mixed operations by Floor 6+. Visit shops to buy upgrades, find treasure rooms for stat boosts, and challenge floor bosses with harder problems. Wrong answers show the correct solution so kids learn as they play — no punishment, just encouragement.

Features

  • 3 character classes with specials
  • 6+ math difficulty tiers
  • Shop and treasure system
  • Encouraging wrong-answer feedback

Controls

  • A/B/C/D to answer
  • Arrow keys to navigate
  • S for special

Snake

Arcade Puzzle Ages 6+

Guide your googly-eyed snake around the grid, gobble up food, and grow as long as you can without crashing. Snake features three difficulty levels — Easy with wall wrapping and a continue, Normal with wall collision, and Hard with spawning obstacles and a reachability check so you never get trapped unfairly. Collect power-ups like Slow-Mo, Magnet, Ghost mode, and Double Points. Hit milestones to unlock rainbow mode, background star fields, and aurora effects. Every eat triggers particles, floating score text, and a satisfying crunch. Compete against your own high score on each difficulty.

Features

  • 4 power-up types
  • Rainbow mode & background evolution
  • 3 difficulty levels
  • Milestone celebrations

Controls

  • Arrow keys or WASD to steer
  • P to pause
  • M to mute

Backyard Baseball

Sports Ages 6+

Step up to the plate in this Home Run Derby featuring adorable bobblehead players with spring-physics heads. Time your swing perfectly to crush baseballs over the outfield fence. Every hit gives instant visual and audio feedback so you know exactly how you did. Three difficulty levels scale pitch speed and timing windows from beginner-friendly to challenging. A distance progress meter with milestone goals gives you something to chase beyond just the score. Home runs trigger slow-motion instant replays, fireworks celebrations, and an organ riff.

Features

  • Timing-based batting with instant feedback
  • 3 difficulty levels (Easy, Normal, Hard)
  • Slow-motion HR replays & fireworks
  • Distance progress meter with milestones
  • Per-difficulty high score leaderboard
  • Tutorial for first-time players

Controls

  • Space to swing
  • Arrow keys for menu
  • P to pause
  • M to mute
