Pac-Man — Free Online Arcade Game

Play Pac-Man free in your browser at Dad Arcade. A loving recreation of the classic arcade maze chase — eat all the pellets, dodge the ghosts, and grab a power pellet to turn the tables.

How to Play

Controls

Arrow keys or WASD to move. P or Esc to pause. M to mute. Enter or Space to start.

Ghosts

Blinky (red) chases you directly. Pinky (pink) tries to ambush ahead of you. Inky (cyan) is unpredictable. Clyde (orange) is shy when close.

Power Pellets

Eat one of the four large flashing dots in the corners and the ghosts turn blue — chase and eat them for big points. Consecutive ghost eats score 200, 400, 800, 1600.
