Chomp Chase — Free Online Arcade Game

Play Chomp Chase free in your browser at Dad Arcade. An original maze-chase game — eat all the pellets, dodge the chasers, and grab a power pellet to turn the tables.

How to Play

Controls

Arrow keys or WASD to move. P or Esc to pause. M to mute. Enter or Space to start.

Chasers

Red chases you directly. Pink tries to ambush ahead of you. Blue is unpredictable. Orange is shy when close.

Power Pellets

Eat one of the four large flashing dots in the corners and the chasers turn blue — chase and eat them for big points. Consecutive chaser eats score 200, 400, 800, 1600.
