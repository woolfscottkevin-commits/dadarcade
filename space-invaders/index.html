Space Invaders — Free Online Arcade Game

Play Space Invaders free in your browser at Dad Arcade. This modern remake features the classic alien-shooting gameplay plus power-ups, boss fights, and a global leaderboard.

About This Game

How to Play

Move your ship with the arrow keys (or A/D) and press spacebar to fire. Destroy all aliens to advance to the next wave. The aliens march side to side and drop down — if they reach your level, it's game over.

Power-Ups

Defeated aliens may drop power-ups: Triple Shot fires three bullets in a spread, Rapid Fire lets you hold spacebar for automatic shooting, and Barrier places a shield in front of your ship that blocks one hit.

Boss Fights

Every 5th wave features a boss battle. Bosses have unique attack patterns including spread shots, aimed bullets, and bombs that explode into fragments. Destroy the boss and its escort fleet to advance.

Leaderboard

When your game ends, enter your 3-letter initials to submit your score to the global leaderboard. Compete with players worldwide for the top 10.
