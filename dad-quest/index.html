About Dad Quest

Dad Quest is a free browser card battler roguelike where suburban chores become tactical problems. Pick Hank, Doug, or Brenda, climb a branching three-act map, and build a deck from attacks, skills, powers, relics, shop finds, and strange neighborhood events.

Each run mixes quick turn-based combat with visible enemy intents, energy management, card rewards, rest sites, and shops. Hank builds Yard Work for lawn-care payoffs, Doug converts Caffeine into explosive office momentum, and Brenda buries enemies under Citations. Defeat the Ultimate HOA President at the end of Act 3 to complete the run.
