How to Play Checkers

  • Goal: capture all of your opponent's pieces, or leave them with no legal move.
  • Moving: tap one of your glowing pieces, then tap a green square. Regular pieces move diagonally forward by one.
  • Jumping: hop diagonally over an opponent's piece into the empty square beyond to capture it (shown in red).
  • Forced jumps: if you can capture, you must. Only capturing pieces will glow.
  • Multi-jumps: if another jump is available after you land, you keep going with the same piece.
  • King me: reach the far row and your piece becomes a ♛ King — it can move and jump both forward and backward.
