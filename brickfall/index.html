Brickfall — Original falling-blocks arcade stacker

Seven shapes drop into a 10-wide pit. Slide them, rotate them, fill rows to clear them. The longer you survive, the faster they fall.

How to play

Keyboard

  • Left / Right — move piece sideways
  • Up — rotate 90° clockwise
  • Down — soft drop (1 point per row)
  • Space — hard drop (2 points per row, locks instantly)
  • P or Esc — pause / resume
  • R — restart on game-over screen

Touch

  • On-screen buttons appear below the playfield on phones and tablets
  • Hold a button to repeat the action

Scoring

  • 1 line: 100 × level
  • 2 lines: 300 × level
  • 3 lines: 500 × level
  • 4 lines (a four-line clear): 800 × level
  • Every 10 lines, level goes up and pieces fall faster
